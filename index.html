Why Are These So Cheap?

Fair question. Here's the honest answer.

We're a real dealer, not a reseller

XBOLTZ is run by an authorized Snapcycle & Mihogo dealer based in Winter Garden, FL. These are genuine units from our own dealer inventory — some still sealed in the factory box, some demo and personal-use bikes — with real spec sheets and real support behind them.

Clearance means clearance

We're clearing inventory to make room. No middlemen, no showroom overhead, no shipping cost baked in. That's how a $2,299 bike ends up at $499 — and why quantities are limited to what's physically in stock.

Questions? Answered.

Are these bikes new or used?

Both, and every listing says which. The Snapcycle Storm is brand new in an unopened factory box (pink, grey or silver), and the Pegasus Lite is brand new. The Snapcycle S1 and Mihogo Air Max are used. The photos shown for those two are manufacturer images of the model — ask us and we'll send real photos of the actual bike before you drive out. Either way you inspect and test ride the exact bike before paying.

How does the free delivery + test ride work?

If you're within roughly 2-3 hours of the Orlando area, we drive the bike to you. You test ride it at your door. Love it? Pay on the spot. Don't? We take it back — zero cost to you.

How do I pay?

Cash or Zelle, in person only — at pickup or delivery, after your test ride. No online payment is taken on this site, ever, and we will never ask you to send money before you have seen the bike in person. If a site that looks like ours asks for your card details, or anyone asks you to wire a deposit up front to hold a bike, it is not us.

Where exactly are you located?

Winter Garden, FL (west Orlando metro). For safety we share exact meeting details when you reserve a bike.

Can you hold a bike for me?

Bikes go to whoever confirms first. Email us to reserve — we'll confirm availability and set a pickup or delivery time, usually same week.

Is there any warranty or support?

Clearance bikes are sold as-is at clearance pricing — but they come from an authorized dealer, so genuine spare parts and repair guidance are available. Ask us about parts support for your model.
